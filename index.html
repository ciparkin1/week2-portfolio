About Me

Hello! My name is Caroline and I am a Rossendale based web developer.

I currenlty work full time as a project manager for the NHS, I love my job and after 12 years in the NHS I feel ready to continue my professional career as a Web Developer. In my younger years I spent time backpacking in New Zealand, Australia, Thailand and Europe. I have an undergraduate degree in Englaish Literature (BA Hons) and a postgraduate degree in Law. For many year I spent weekends selling my handmade items at a Makers Market's in and around Manchester. I even went as far as to establish my own makers market in Rossendale on Rawtenstall market. Creativity is what I come back to again and again and I am excited to have found a career path that supports my creativity. Cant wait to get started!

My hobbies include; walking, drawing, gardening, paddle boarding and as of now, coding!
